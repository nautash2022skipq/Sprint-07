Kevin Mitnick is a former American computer hacker who gained notoriety in the 1980s and 1990s for his high-profile computer security exploits. Mitnick's hacking skills allowed him to break into a number of large corporations' computer systems, leading to theft of sensitive information and other malicious activities. He was eventually arrested and served five years in prison for his crimes. After his release, Mitnick became a consultant and author, using his expertise to advise companies on how to improve their security measures. Today, he is considered one of the most famous hackers in history, and his exploits continue to be studied and discussed in the cybersecurity community.
